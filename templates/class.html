ACCA

  • · Operating profit margin=PBIT(profit before interest and tax)/sales×100%
  • · Gross profit margin=profit margin/sales×100%
  • · Capital employed=shareholder’s equity+long-term debts=total assets-current liability
  • · ROCE=operating profit margin×asset turnover=PBIT/capital employed×100%
  • · Quick ratio=(current asset-inventory)/current liability
  • · Payable payment days=trade payable/purchase(or cost of sales)×365
  • · Gearing ratio=total long term debt/(shareholder’equity+long-term debt)×365
  • · Interest cover=PBIT/interest charges
  • · Debt ratio=total debts/total assets×365
